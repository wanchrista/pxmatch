About BikeRack


While on vacation, tourists may find it difficult and expensive to rent cars and purchase insurance if they lack an international driving license. Public transit can also be difficult to navigate in foreign languages. Public bike sharing systems breaches the barrier of other expensive transit options and offers a healthier alternative to exploration.

BikeRack allows users to pre-plan their destinations by locating city bike stations worldwide, allowing for an eco-friendly, affordable method of transit while travelling.


Usage


Use our search feature and enter a city, country or company name. BikeRack will display a list of bike sharing stations based on your input.
By selecting city or country, view all bike sharing stations within the vicinity.
By selecting company, view all locations that the company services.

Optionally, create an account with us by signing up, or log in if you already have an account to access additional features!


Note: Search country by its abbreviation.



BikeRack is a project created by a team of 4 university students at the University of Toronto.
